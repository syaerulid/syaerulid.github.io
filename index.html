How to Prepare Yourself as a Company Owner in Facing an FDA Recall (R lang)

This project entails a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) of FDA recall patterns. By analyzing which months experience the highest recall frequencies and employing keyword extraction techniques, including unigrams and bigrams, we pinpoint the top 10 reasons for product recalls. Additionally, we visually represent the distribution of product types subject to recalls. This data-driven approach equips companies with actionable insights, enabling them to proactively prepare for potential recalls and safeguard their products.
